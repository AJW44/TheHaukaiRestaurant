RESERVATIONS


For parties of six or more, we recommend making reservations at least two weeks in advance. For walk-ins, we only seat parties on a first come, first served basis.

Please note that The Haukai Restaurant is open from Wednesday to Sunday inclusive from 6.00pm to 11.00pm.

The Haukai Restaurant will endeavour to meet all special dietry requirements, but must be noted on the Reservation Form.

When filling out the Reservation form, the scroll bar will need to be used to complete the entire form. The use of the Tab button can be used to navigate between each section. An asterix will appear when information is needed to be compulsararily entered.
